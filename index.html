Contest Prizes

The following prizes will be distributed to the top 100 players (per the competition leaderboard which will display on this page) at the end of the contest:

  • 1st Place: 4,096 Sui
  • 2nd Place: 2,048 Sui
  • 3rd Place: 1,024 Sui
  • 4th Place: 512 Sui
  • 5th Place: 256 Sui
  • 6th Place: 128 Sui
  • 7th - 9th Place: 64 Sui
  • 10th -24th Place: 32 Sui
  • 25th - 49th Place: 16 Sui
  • 50th - 100th Place: 8 Sui
Contest Rules

The following are the rules of the mut3 8192 contest, sponsored by the Sui Foundation:

  • US residents are not eligible
  • All games must be created after June 20th, 2023 at 4pm UTC
  • Leaderboard will be locked and contest will end at 4pm UTC on June 27th, 2023
  • Valid games will be displayed in your "Your Contest Games" section
  • All winners must provide a valid email and go through a KYC "Know Your Customer" process to receive winnings.
  • Each individual can only win a prize for one game, their highest scoring game of the competition
